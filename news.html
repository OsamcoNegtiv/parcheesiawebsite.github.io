Massive Data Breach

April 1st, 2025

Written by Alisha Valentine

A massive data breach containing the information of alost every citizen has been reported. It seems to be related to the recent census performed by the extra-governmental entity known only as "The Character Archival Sheet." Work is still being done to find the exact extent of the breach, but it is known that important data for virtually everyone living in the country has been leaked, including usernames, passwords, and government assigned IDs. More information will be posted as it comes to light.
